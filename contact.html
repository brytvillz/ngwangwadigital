Common Questions

How much does a website cost?

Basic business websites start from ₦150,000. The final price depends on your specific needs, number of pages, features, and design complexity. Contact us for a free quote tailored to your business.

How long does it take to build a website?

Most business websites are delivered within 2-4 weeks from the day you approve the design. We work fast without compromising quality. NgwaNgwa means "quick quick" in Igbo — that's our promise.

Do you provide support after launch?

Yes! Every project includes 1 month of free support after launch. We'll fix any bugs, answer questions, and make sure everything runs smoothly. After that, ongoing support packages are available.

Can you help with SEO?

Absolutely. All our websites come with basic SEO setup included. For businesses that want to rank higher on Google, we offer dedicated SEO services starting from ₦100,000/month.

What makes NgwaNgwa Digital different?

We understand Nigerian business realities. We build websites that work on slow networks. We price fairly. We answer our phones. We deliver on time. And we're building our own products, not just doing client work — we're innovators, not just service providers.
